Chicken Inasal

Chicken inasal is a grilled chicken dish originating from the Illongo region of the Philippines. The chicken is marinated in a combination of unique spices. The marinade and basting sauce makes this grilled chicken packed with flavour.

Nutrition Info

Meal prep: 1 hour

Cooking time: 18 minutes

Total time: 1 hour 18 minutes

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken cut into serving pieces
  • 2 tablespoons minced ginger
  • 2 tablespoons garlic minced
  • 3/4 cup lemongrass chopped
  • 1 cup coconut vinegar
  • 1/2 cup calamansi juice
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup lemon soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground black pepper
  • Basting Sauce:
  • 3 tablespoons annatto oil atsuete oil
  • 1/2 cup butter melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon calamansi juice

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, place and season the chicken with lemongrass, salt, ground black pepper, ginger, garlic, brown sugar, vinegar, lemon-lime soda, and lemon juice.
  2. Stir the mixture and marinade for one to three hours.
  3. In a bowl, add margarine, annatto oil, salt, and lemon juice than stir. Set aside.
  4. Grill chicken and use basting sauce on both sodes with a brush.
  5. Transfer the grilled chicken to serving plates.
  6. Serve with yellow turmeric or white rice and sinamak.
