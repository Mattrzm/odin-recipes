Passion Fruit Mango Bubble Tea

Bubble tea or Boba is a unique drink that combines various flavours and textures. This tropical flavour uses passion fruit to compliment the black tea.

Nutrition Info

Meal prep: 10 minutes

Cooking time: 50 minutes

Total time: 1 hour

Servings: 1

Yield: 1 servings

Ingredients

  • Bubbles/boba:
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 5 cups and 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup tapioca pearls
  • Tea:
  • 5 passionfruits
  • 2 cups black tea, chilled
  • 3 tbsp sugar or maple syrup or simple syrup
  • 1 cup ice or 1 cup frozen passionfruit juices cubes or 1 cup frozen mango

Steps

  1. To make the boba begin with a simple syrup, combine 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 cup water. Heat until the sugar is fully dissolved. Transfer to a jar.
  2. Rinse the pot than boil 5 cups of water. Once at a boil, add the tapioca pearls. Cook on high heat for twenty minutes, stirring once very ten minutes. Lower to a simmer and cook until soft and chewy, about five to ten minutes, stirring every ten minutes. Remove from heat and let set in the water for thirty minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water. Add to the jar with syrup and allow it to soak up the sugar.
  3. Half the passionfruits and strain pulp over a mesh sieve to get the juice. You may yield about 1/4 to 1/3 cup of juice depending on the quality of your passionfruit.
  4. If you don't want to use syrup, add 3 tbsp sugar to your black tea once you brew it so it will dissolve. Otherwise, use maple syrup, simple syrup, or honey.
  5. In a blender, add the passionfruit juice, sweetened black tea, and frozen component. You can use ice, frozen passionfruit juice (freeze into ice cube trays), or 1 cup frozen mango. Frozen passionfruit juice is ideal but frozen mango adds the tropical flavour. Blend on high until it becomes slushy.
  6. To serve, add 1/4 cup cooked boba with syrup from the jar to the glass. Next, pour passionfruit tea slushy on top and serve.
